Why Photus?

We started as a little team, offering our services of taking photos 10 years ago, so, from that day, our skills have been improved and we decided to found Photus, a photo as a service, providing to our clients shots with good quality and ensuring their best moments not only being in her minds.

Our team is very friendly and will help you with suggestions on how to take the photo and why. If you need, feel free to ask anything anytime.

Quality is a must have to us, so you can relax when we are at our service, the quality always will be the primary goal, you will not have to bother with that again.
